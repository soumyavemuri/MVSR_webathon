With record 6,988 new cases, Tamil Nadu's Covid-19 tally crosses 2 lakh

Tamil Nadu's Covid-19 overall caseload on Saturday crossed the grim milestone of 2 lakh-mark as the state registered record 6,988 new cases in the last 24 hours. This is the biggest single-day spike in new cases the state has witnessed so far. The total number of positive cases in the state stands at 2,06,737, including 1,51,055 recoveries and 3,409 deaths.
